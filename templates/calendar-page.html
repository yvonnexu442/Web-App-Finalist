Calendar

The calendar to the right displays GWCCA booked events, including future events that have already been booked in advance.

Add & Remove

The calendar page has a Google calendar embedded. In order to add or remove events, the user can do so here, and the results are automatically synced to the Calendar page. For further help about the Google calendar, click here.

Attention

Only events that have been manually entered to the Google Calendar will be loaded. The calendar is not connected to any GWCCA database or this Web-App's back-end database.
